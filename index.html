Our Origin

Sunrise Roastery began in the highlands of Ethiopia, where our founders discovered a love for bold, natural coffee. Since 2005, we've been sourcing the finest beans and roasting them to perfection to share the authentic coffee experience with the world.

Our Values

  • Quality: Only the best beans, expertly roasted.
  • Sustainability: Supporting ethical farming practices and eco-friendly packaging.
  • Community: Building strong relationships with farmers and customers alike.
  • Passion: We love what we do and share it in every cup.
